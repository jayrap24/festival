  • When do tickets go on sale?

    Presales for The xx fans and BBK ticket holders begin on Wednesday 11th April at 12PM local time. General on sale begins Thursday 12th April at 10AM local time. The package option (BBK Live weekend pass and admission to Jamie xx at The Guggenheim) will be publicly available for purchase starting on Wednesday 11th April at 10AM local, when the presales open.
